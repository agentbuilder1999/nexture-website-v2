The Future of Capsule Endoscopy

TheraSeus™ is a breakthrough AI platform designed to transform how clinicians analyze capsule endoscopy studies. By automatically filtering irrelevant frames and detecting critical findings with clinical-grade accuracy, TheraSeus™ reduces review time by 90% while maintaining diagnostic excellence.

HOW IT WORKS

Three Simple Steps

1

Upload

Import capsule endoscopy video directly from your existing equipment. Supports all major formats (AVI, MP4, DICOM) and manufacturers.

2

Analyze

TheraSeus™ AI processes each frame in real-time, filtering irrelevant images and detecting bleeding, lesions, and other findings with 99.2% accuracy.

3

Report

Review AI-annotated findings and auto-generated clinical reports. Export to PDF, DICOM, or integrate directly with your EMR system.

CAPABILITIES

Clinical-Grade AI

Image Filtering

Automatically removes non-diagnostic frames including gastric juice, bubbles, debris, and motion blur. Reduces 50,000+ frames to ~5,000 clinically relevant images.

  • • Adaptive region-based filtering
  • • Real-time processing
  • • Customizable sensitivity

Bleeding Detection

Deep learning models trained on 500,000+ annotated frames detect active bleeding, blood clots, fresh blood, and altered blood.

  • • 99.2% sensitivity
  • • 97.8% specificity
  • • Confidence scoring

Lesion Identification

Detects ulcers, erosions, polyps, tumors, and angiodysplasia with clinical-grade accuracy.

  • • Multi-class detection
  • • Anatomical localization
  • • Temporal tracking

Report Generation

Auto-generated structured reports with annotated findings, timestamps, confidence scores, and frame captures.

  • • DICOM-compatible
  • • Customizable templates
  • • EMR integration
