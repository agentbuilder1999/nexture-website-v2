OUR SERVICES

TheraSeus™ AI Platform

Revolutionizing Capsule Endoscopy Analysis

TheraSeus™ is an AI-powered platform that automates the analysis of capsule endoscopy videos, reducing review time by 90% while maintaining 99.2% diagnostic accuracy.

CAPABILITIES

Core Features

🔍

AI Image Filtering

Our proprietary AI model analyzes each frame in real-time, automatically filtering out irrelevant images such as gastric juice, bubbles, and debris. This reduces the average 50,000-frame capsule study to approximately 5,000 clinically relevant frames.

  • • Adaptive filtering based on anatomical region
  • • Real-time processing (6 minutes per case)
  • • Customizable sensitivity thresholds
🎯

Bleeding Detection

State-of-the-art deep learning models detect active bleeding, blood clots, and fresh/altered blood with 99.2% sensitivity and 97.8% specificity.

  • • Multi-class classification (active, fresh, altered blood)
  • • Confidence scoring for each detection
  • • Temporal tracking across video segments
📊

Automated Reporting

Generate comprehensive clinical reports with annotated findings, timestamps, confidence scores, and relevant frame captures—ready for physician review and patient records.

  • • Structured DICOM-compatible reports
  • • Customizable report templates
  • • Export to PDF, DICOM, or HL7 formats
