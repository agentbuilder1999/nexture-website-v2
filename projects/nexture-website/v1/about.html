ABOUT NEXTURE

Pioneering the Future of Medical AI

Our Mission

To empower healthcare professionals with AI-driven tools that enhance diagnostic accuracy, reduce clinical workload, and improve patient outcomes—making advanced medical imaging accessible to hospitals worldwide.

OUR VALUES

What Drives Us

🚀

Innovation

🎯

Precision

Clinical accuracy is paramount. Our models are rigorously validated to meet the highest standards of diagnostic performance.

Accessibility

Advanced AI should be available to all healthcare facilities, not just major research hospitals. We design for scalability and ease of integration.

OUR JOURNEY

Milestones

2020

Founded

Nexture founded in Christchurch, New Zealand with seed funding from Ministry of Awesome and angel investors.

2022

First Clinical Trial

Successful pilot study with University of Otago showing 89% time reduction and 98.5% accuracy in capsule endoscopy analysis.

2023

NVIDIA Inception

Accepted into NVIDIA Inception program. Partnered with Google Cloud and AWS for enterprise-grade infrastructure.

2024

TheraSeus™ Launch

Commercial launch of TheraSeus™ platform. First deployments in New Zealand and Australian hospitals.

2026

Global Expansion

Expanding to North America and Europe. Pursuing CE Mark and FDA 510(k) clearance. 20+ hospital partnerships.
