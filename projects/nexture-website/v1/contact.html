Frequently Asked Questions

TheraSeus™ offers three pricing tiers: Pilot ($135/case for up to 50 cases/month), Clinical ($99/case for 100+ cases/month), and Enterprise (custom pricing). There are no upfront costs or hardware requirements. Contact our sales team for a detailed quote tailored to your facility's needs.

TheraSeus™ is compatible with all major capsule endoscopy systems (Medtronic PillCam™, Olympus EndoCapsule, CapsoVision CapsoCam, etc.). We offer cloud-based deployment for rapid setup, or on-premise installation for facilities requiring full data control. Integration typically takes 1-2 weeks and includes training for your team.

Yes. TheraSeus™ is fully HIPAA-compliant with SOC 2 Type II certification. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We are currently pursuing CE Mark certification (EU) and FDA 510(k) clearance (USA). TheraSeus™ is registered with Medsafe in New Zealand and TGA in Australia.

For cloud deployment, most facilities are operational within 1-2 weeks from contract signing. On-premise deployments typically take 3-4 weeks. The timeline includes system setup, data integration testing, staff training, and pilot case reviews. We provide dedicated onboarding support throughout the process.

All plans include email support with response times based on your tier (24-48 hours for Pilot, 12-24 hours for Clinical, 24/7 for Enterprise). We provide comprehensive training materials, video tutorials, and regular webinars. Clinical and Enterprise customers receive priority support and dedicated account management.
