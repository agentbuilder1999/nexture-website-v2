## 2. Services

### 🔍 Intelligent Frame Analysis
Nexture automatically filters redundant and low-quality frames from capsule endoscopy studies, reducing review time by up to 60% without sacrificing diagnostic completeness. Clinicians see only what matters.

### 🩺 Lesion & Bleeding Detection
Powered by deep learning models trained on validated gastrointestinal datasets, Nexture flags potential vascular abnormalities and bleeding sites with confidence scores — surfacing critical findings before manual review.

### 📊 Structured Clinical Reports
Every study generates a structured summary with annotated frames, lesion location estimates, and severity indicators — ready to integrate with your existing PACS and reporting workflow.

### ⚙️ Configurable AI Sensitivity
Adjust detection thresholds to match your department's clinical risk tolerance. Conservative, standard, or aggressive filtering modes let your team stay in control of every diagnostic decision.
